Brazil To Win Qatar World Cup 2022

By: Miguel Rojas

The FIFA (Federation Internationale De Football Association) World Cup is an international tournament played among thirty-two mens' teams. This event is held every four years and it is always in a different location.

For example the next World Cup in 2026 will be held in the USA, Mexico and Canada. With the 2022 World Cup on its' way there would have now been 22 World Cups played.

The last World Cup was won by the French national team. (6-0-1). They had a good run up to the end of the tournament. They added their second world cup trophy to their collection of trophies back in 2018. Their last world cup won was back in 1998. The 1998 world cup was played in France. It must have been a great feeling winning the world cup in their own country.

I think the 2022 World Cup is going to be won by Brazil. I believe this year they will win because of how much young talent they have like, Vinicius Jr, Antony and Raphinha. These young players are inspiring the senior players of the team to push harder for the trophy. These senior players (Alisson, Thiago Silva and Neymar Jr.) know what it feels like to not win a world cup and losing in quarter finals.

I am sure they will tell their younger players to play with all their might to represent their country with heart. Last time Brazil lifted the World Cup trophy was back in 2002 in Korea, Japan. They can certainly win the World Cup this year in Qatar.
