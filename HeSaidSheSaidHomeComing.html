He Said/She Said: Homecoming Proposals

He Said By: Qualik Johnson

Being the one to build up the courage to ask someone to Homecoming allows a person to take action and further improve the relationship with the person he/she is interested in.

Wouldn't you rather plan a surprise with flowers or a poster to make Homecoming more meaningful and memorable?

Making a plan to surprise someone for Homecoming would help possibly take your relationship to the next level.

Using Homecoming to surprise someone for the upcoming event as a way to express how you feel about them is a way to make it even more exciting than it was before.

Taking the opportunity to ask someone to the dance in-person could help the questioner gain a confidence boost. Asking someone in-person will provide the person asking with the pleasure of seeing someone happy and excited to be asked in such a dramatic and thoughtful way.

Even though there is the possibility of getting rejected a person should still take the chance that their Hoco-proposal could be accepted by the person he/she plans on asking.

It is better to ask someone to Homecoming rather than waiting to be asked because of the potential joy it will bring.

She Said By: Neyri Fragoso

In modern society it does not really matter who asks whom to homecoming. There is no pressure to you go with someone or not. It just depends on who you are as a person. Some prefer to ask someone out, others prefer to get asked out and others simply go solo.

It also depends how the person asks the other to homecoming. Whether it is a public hoco-proposal or more of a private proposal.

Many factors come with not wanting to ask someone to homecoming, one being the fear of rejection and the embarrassment that may come along after. Talking about it with the other person may be a good idea so that both mutually agree and are aware in case either person prefers to do a Hoco-proposal.

Many like the idea of getting asked to homecoming because it is a nice once-in-a-lifetime experience. Either way getting asked or asking someone to homecoming can be a ride of emotions.

At the end of the day it is a unique opportunity, so a person might as well make the best out of it whether you plan to ask someone, bor solo.
