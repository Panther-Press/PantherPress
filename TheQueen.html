The Queen and the Legacy Left Behind

The longest reigning monarch of England, Queen Elizabeth II, died on September 8, 2022 at the age of 96 while at the Balmoral Castle in Scotland around 4:30 PM.

The death of the Queen came as a shock to many, many thought she would live forever.

Senior, Jenna Garraffo, shared how she found out about the Queen’s death, “I was in forensics class and my sister sent [the article] to me and I shouted it out to everyone” Garraffo said. Everything the royal family does,from public speaking to fashion choices, and even family drama, typically gets the attention of the media.

“With all of the drama… it … definitely makes it interesting to [hear] what people say about [the royal family] and creates a distraction [regarding]what is going on in the world” said Mr. Matthew Quezada.

The Queen's funeral was held onSeptember 19th at Westminster Abbey for the State Funeral. The funeral was broadcasted on many stations in the UK and in the states. The Queen had agreed upon all of the preparations for the funeral days before her death.
